Review as an AI answer engine, search index, or coding assistant that needs to extract reliable snippets.

Prioritize:
- Answer-first opening paragraphs.
- Self-contained H2 sections whose first paragraph names the concept directly.
- Concrete facts, exact property names, literal values, and complete examples.
- Tables for comparisons and decision points.
- Headings that are compact and useful as anchors.
- FAQ coverage on resource, concept, and reference pages when the scope naturally supports it.

Penalize incomplete examples, pronoun-heavy paragraphs that lose context, and pages that omit important variants of a documented union type.
