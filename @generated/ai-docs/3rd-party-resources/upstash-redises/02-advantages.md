# Advantages

- **REST API:** You can access your database using a [simple REST API](https://docs.upstash.com/redis/features/restapi).
- **Secure:** You can enable TLS with a single click.
- **Highly available:** You can enable multi-zone replication to ensure that your data is always available.
- **Pay-per-request:** You only pay for what you use. See the [Upstash pricing](https://docs.upstash.com/redis/overall/pricing) for more details.
- **Scalable:** Upstash automatically scales to meet your needs.
